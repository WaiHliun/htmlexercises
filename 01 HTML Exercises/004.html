Business Letter

Dear Ms.Nandar

Thank you so much for taking the time to meet with me to discuss selling my handmade sweaters in your shop. As I mention in our conversation, I've been a customer of your shop since I used my third-grade allowance to buy my very first pair of knitting needles.

Thanks
Thura
